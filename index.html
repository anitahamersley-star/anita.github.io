About CalmShare

CalmShare exists because secure file sharing in healthcare has become unnecessarily complicated.

Many clinicians are forced to choose between systems that are:

  • Too technical
  • Too expensive
  • Too cluttered
  • Designed for large enterprises, not real clinical work

CalmShare is built specifically for allied‑health professionals who want a calm, respectful experience for clients, confidence that sensitive information is handled appropriately, and a system that “just works” without ongoing management.

It focuses on what matters - and leaves everything else out.

Frequently asked questions

Is CalmShare compliant with Australian privacy requirements?

CalmShare is designed to support compliance with the Australian Privacy Principles. Data is hosted in Australia, and the platform is built with privacy and confidentiality in mind.

Is CalmShare suitable for client use?

Yes. CalmShare is designed to be simple and client-friendly, including for clients who are not comfortable with technology.

What happens after I subscribe?

After subscribing, you receive access to your CalmShare account and can begin sharing documents with clients straight away.

Can clients upload documents to me?

Yes. CalmShare supports secure two-way document sharing between you and your clients.

Do I need to replace my existing practice management system?

No. CalmShare is designed to work alongside your existing systems and focuses only on secure document sharing.

How is billing handled? Can I cancel?

Subscriptions are billed monthly, and you can cancel at any time through your billing portal.

Is CalmShare suitable for clinics?

Yes. CalmShare supports both solo practitioners and clinics, with plans designed for each.
